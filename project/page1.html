About the Artist

Meet Olivia Scopa!

Olivia Scopa is a nineteen year old aspiring painter, graphic designer, knitter, and art student based in Boston, Massachusetts.

She is currently receiving a B.F.A in Graphic Design at Lesley University College of Art and Design.

Her current project, Cosmic Knits, focuses on the virtue of knitting as a mode of art one can wear, and share with the world. Her colorful and quirky knits are tailored to complement your own aura, and add a vibrant hue to any closet. Originally making individualized sweaters for her friends, she now hopes to share her Cosmic Knits with the rest of the galaxy.
